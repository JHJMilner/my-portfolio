Julia Milner, UX Researcher, Service Designer

Hi. I'm Julia.

I'm a design researcher with a passion for bringing meaningful improvements to people's lives through technology.

For me, UX research is about more than improving basic usability. It's about making sure that the right things get built, and that users are seeing real value.

Organizations often stall not from a lack of vision, but from trying to navigate complexity without a map. I take questions and find insights to map a clear path forward. Together, we can get your team going in the right direction.

Replace assumptions with evidence.

Whether you're defining a product roadmap, untangling a complex service experience, or aligning a team around what to build next, the work starts with shared understanding. Clarity is not a luxury, it's a prerequisite.
